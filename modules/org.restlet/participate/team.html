Alternative funding

When Restlet S.A.S. founded the open source Restlet project, it made the choice of Professional Open Source. It means that it contributes most of the development effort, pays the core developers and the hosting expenses, provides technical leadership and integrates contributions from the community like bug reports, patches and documentation improvements.

Some individuals are doing personal work or are exploring the technology at an early stage and would still like to contribute to the funding of the project. If you fit this description, you can make a voluntary donation via PayPal:

Finally, you can also help us by buying one of the books that we recommend. If you buy a book right after following the hyperlink from our page, we will receive a commission back from the book seller.

Thanks in advance for your interest and your support!
